Bio

I am an ML Research Resident at Yandex Research × HSE Joint Laboratory working on LLM, LLM Efficiency and Reasoning.

Previously I worked as a Data Scientist at OneMarketData, an ML Engineer at VK.com and an SWE Intern at Yandex.

I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science (Applied Mathematics and Programming) and my Master’s Degree in Computer Science (Programming and Artificial Intelligence) from ITMO University, Saint Petersburg.

Research Interests

Large Language Models (LLMs), LLM Efficiency and Capabilities, Reasoning, Language Model Architectures, Reinforcement Learning.
